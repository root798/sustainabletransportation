About

The CLEAR-ATS model simulates an Automated Transport System from 2024 to 2080, tracking:

  • Vehicle populations
  • Infrastructure upgrades
  • Power consumption
  • CO₂ emissions

All power values are in kWh and emissions in kg CO₂.
