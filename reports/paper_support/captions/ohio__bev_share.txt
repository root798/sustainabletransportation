Ohio EV Fraction trajectory under the baseline scenario. Solid line: p50 median; shaded band: p05–p95 Monte-Carlo range (N = 200 samples, seed 42). The interpretation boundary at 2031 marks where the p05–p95 width exceeds 150 % of the median; values before this year are quantitatively interpretable, values from this year onward should be read as a scenario envelope rather than point projections. Ohio's modelled BEV share does not saturate within the 2024–2092 horizon and does not reach a within-horizon peak, so neither the cap-artefact caveat nor the horizon-edge peak caveat applies here.
