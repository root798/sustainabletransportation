Ohio ATS Emissions (kg CO2) trajectory under the baseline scenario. Solid line: p50 median; shaded band: p05–p95 Monte-Carlo range (N = 200 samples, seed 42). The interpretation boundary at 2031 marks where the p05–p95 width exceeds 150 % of the median; values before this year are quantitatively interpretable, values from this year onward should be read as a scenario envelope rather than point projections. Modelled peak year 2076; modelled turning year not reached within horizon. Peak and turning years are attributed to the deterministic central trajectory (footprint_model.py --mc 0). The baseline MC ensemble is mixed on turning-year achievement: 87 of 200 runs reach the 50 %-of-peak threshold before 2092 (achieved_fraction 0.435); the conditional MC p50 across those 87 runs is 2081. This conditional figure is disclosed here and in the metrics-quantiles CSV only and is not cited as an unconditional paper result. Horizon-edge note: the modelled peak lies within the last 20 years of the 2024–2092 simulation horizon; interpret as a within-horizon extremum, not an asymptote.
