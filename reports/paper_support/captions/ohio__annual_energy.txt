Ohio ATS Total Power (kWh) trajectory under the baseline scenario. Solid line: p50 median; shaded band: p05–p95 Monte-Carlo range (N = 200 samples, seed 42). The interpretation boundary at 2031 marks where the p05–p95 width exceeds 150 % of the median; values before this year are quantitatively interpretable, values from this year onward should be read as a scenario envelope rather than point projections. Annual energy demand in this panel does not reach a within-horizon peak, so the horizon-edge peak caveat applied to the emissions panel is not applicable here.
