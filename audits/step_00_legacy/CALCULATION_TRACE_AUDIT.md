| Annual ATS energy demand | TransportModel.run_simulation() writes ATS Total Power (kWh). | sum(power.values()) after _calculate_power() accumulates sensing, computing, and communication energy for ECAV, ICEAV, and STI cohorts. | Fleet turnover, cohort efficiency improvements, fixed automation-level shares, config consumption tables. | Corrected from power to annual energy demand because the stored quantity is a yearly total. |
| Annual ATS CO2 emissions | TransportModel._calculate_emissions() and run_simulation() write ATS Emissions (kg CO2). | ECAV electricity * (f_clean*e_clean + (1-f_clean)*e_fossil) + ICEAV gasoline * e_gasoline + STI electricity * same grid factor. | Two-bin electricity mix, fixed gasoline emission factor, no upstream lifecycle terms. | Accurate after relabeling as annual emissions. |
| BEV share of modeled stock | TransportModel._ev_fraction() and run_simulation() write EV Fraction. | Initial value is total_ev/total_cars; future value follows the active adoption curve with growth_rates.ev. | Current code treats total_ev as BEV-only and excludes PHEVs. | Corrected from ambiguous EV share to explicit BEV-share wording. |
| Modeled low-carbon electricity share | TransportModel._update_quantities() writes Clean Energy Fraction. | f_clean_t = min(initial_f_clean * (1 + clean_energy_growth_rate) ** t, 1.0). | Scenario-conditioned non-fossil share, not a live EIA feed. | Corrected from ambiguous clean fraction wording. |
| Cumulative emissions | compute_turning_metrics(). | float(df['ATS Emissions (kg CO2)'].sum()) over the simulation horizon. | Simple discrete sum; no non-utility phases. | Accurate after derived-formula caveat was made explicit. |
| Peak year | compute_turning_metrics(). | peak_year = year at argmax ATS emissions. | Purely scenario-dependent and deterministic. | Accurate after page marked it as derived, not measured. |
| Turning year | compute_turning_metrics(). | First year after peak where ATS emissions are <= 50% of peak. | Uses a 50%-of-peak threshold. | Accurate after exact threshold formula was shown. |
| Subsystem energy decomposition | TransportModel._calculate_power() and stored subsystem columns. | Cohort-specific sensing, computing, and communication energy are accumulated separately for ECAV, ICEAV, and STI. | Automation-level shares fixed by config; efficiency improvements only reduce computing in the current implementation. | Corrected to consistent subsystem names: sensing, computing, communication. |
| State comparison outputs | Page 03 runs run_transport_simulation(load_runtime_config(region, selected_policy)) independently per region. | No cross-region substitution; each chart is a fresh deterministic run from its own runtime config. | U.S. Average is synthetic. | Corrected with explicit synthetic-region labeling and BEV/low-carbon wording. |
